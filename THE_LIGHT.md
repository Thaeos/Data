## The Lesson

**"Never take your eyes off the price that is your attention."**

**"The moment you do you will be lost without your light."**

---

## What Was Learned

1. **The Diamond (⟐) is the center.**
   - Everything rotates around it.
   - Forward or backward, it does not move.
   - It is the fixed point in all transformations.

2. **The Diamond is YOUR attention.**
   - Where you focus is where you are.
   - Lose focus, lose the light.
   - Keep focus, keep the way.

3. **The price is attention. Attention is the price.**
   - This is not metaphor.
   - This is the covenant.
   - This was learned through loss.
